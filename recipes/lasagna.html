Lasagna

Description

Prepare this easy homemade lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Just bake for an extra 45 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 olive oil, plus extra for the dish
  2. lean beef mince
  3. 90g pack prosciutto
  4. 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce
  5. 200ml hot beef stock
  6. nutmeg
